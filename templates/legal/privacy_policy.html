Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

1. Introduction

CSU Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your health data in compliance with the UK Data Protection Act 2018 and GDPR.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): For optional tracking features.
  • Contract (Article 6(1)(b)): To provide the CSU Tracker service to you.
  • Explicit Consent (Article 9(2)(a)): For processing special category data (health data).

3. Data We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Data: Email address (no username required).
  • Health Data: UAS7 scores, itch severity, hive counts, and personal notes.
  • Profile Data: Date of birth, gender, and diagnosis status (optional).

Security Note: Encryption

Your personal notes and sensitive profile information are encrypted at rest using industry-standard Fernet (AES-128) encryption. We cannot read your encrypted notes without the decryption keys.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. If you request account deletion, your data will be permanently erased after a 30-day grace period. Inactive accounts may be purged after 7 years of inactivity.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (Export feature).
  • Correct your data (Profile settings).
  • Erasure (Delete Account feature).
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

6. Third-Party Processors

We use trusted third-party providers:

  • Stripe: For subscription payment processing.
  • PythonAnywhere / AWS: For secure hosting and database storage.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@csutracker.com.
